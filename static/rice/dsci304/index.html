Counter-Strike: Global Offensive

Counter-Strike is a first-person competitive video game and one of the largest in terms of the number of active players. Many professional teams compete in professional leagues and tournaments around the globe. In this project, we investigate patterns from thousands of professional game records.

Most Represented Countries

First, we investigate which countries have more players competing in the professional scene. For this, we take a look at all professional games played from November 2015 to March 2020, and note at how many unique professional players have played at least one game per each country.
